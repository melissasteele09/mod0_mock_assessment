Making a Sandwich
1 Grab plate and utensils
  1.1 Get Plate
    1.1.1 Open cabinet
    1.1.2 Reach up
    1.1.3 Select plate
    1.1.4 Close cabinet
    1.1.5 Go to counter
    1.1.6 Set plate down
  1.2 Get butter knife
    1.2.1 Open drawer
    1.2.2 Pick up butter knife
    1.2.3 Close drawer
    1.2.4 Go to counter where plate is
    1.2.5 Set down butter knife
2 Gather toppings
  2.1 Mustard
    2.1.1 Open fridge
    2.1.2 Search for mustard
    2.1.3 Find mustard
    2.1.4 Pick up mustard
    2.1.5 Balance mustard in arm
    2.1.6 Look for more ingredients
  2.2 Mayo
    2.2.1 Search fridge for mayo
    2.2.2 Find mayo
    2.2.3 Pick up mayo
    2.2.4 Walk to counter
    2.2.5 Place mayo and mustard on counter with plate and utensils
  2.3 Lettuce
    2.3.1 Return to fridge
    2.3.2 Search for lettuce
    2.3.3 Pick up lettuce
    2.3.4 Walk to counter
    2.3.5 Place lettuce on counter
  2.4 Tomato
  2.5 Pickles
3 Toast Bread
  3.1 Get bread
    3.1.1 Walk to bread drawer
    3.1.2 Open drawer
    3.1.3 Pick up bread bag
    3.1.4 Close drawer
    3.1.5 Walk to counter
    3.1.6 Place bread with other ingredients
  3.2 Open bread bag
    3.2.1 Grab bag
    3.2.2 Untwist the tie
  3.3 Select two pieces of bread
    3.3.1 Reach in to bag
    3.3.2 Grab bread
  3.4 Walk to toaster
  3.5 Place bread in toaster
    3.5.1 Push down lever
    3.5.2 Wait
  3.6 Get toast when it pops up
  3.7 Walk to counter
  3.8 Place toast on plate
4 Assemble Sandwich
  4.1 Separate the two pieces of toast
  4.2 Add mayo
    4.2.1 Open jar of mayo
    4.2.2 Pick up butter knife
    4.2.3 Stick knife in mayo
    4.2.4 Remove knife
    4.2.5 Wipe contents onto toast
  4.3 Add mustard
    4.3.1 Shake mustard to get juices mixed in
    4.3.2 Open top of mustard
    4.3.3 Squeeze mustard onto toast
  4.4 Add lettuce
    4.4.1 Pull of a couple leafs of lettuce
    4.4.2 Wash lettuce
      4.4.2.1 Walk to sink
      4.4.2.2 Turn on water
      4.4.2.3 Put lettuce under water
      4.4.2.4 Turn off water
    4.4.3 Pat lettuce dry
    4.4.4 Place lettuce onto sandwich
  4.5 Add tomato
  4.6 Add pickles
  4.7 Top with last piece of toast
